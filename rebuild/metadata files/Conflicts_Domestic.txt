Working Paper CLIO-INFRA: Internal armed conflicts 1500-2000
1. Title
	- Armed internal conflicts, 1500-2000.
2. Author(s)
	- Peter Brecke (original data), Peter Foldvari (conversion to binary data)
3. Production date
	- 14-01-2013
4. Version
	- 1st version.
5. Variable group(s)
	- Political
6. Variable(s)
	- Occurrence of armed conflicts (internal)
7. Unit of analysis
	- binary variable (1 or 0)
8. Keywords (5)
	- armed conflicts, revolt, revolution, uprising, rebellion, civil war
9. Abstract (200 words)
- Armed conflicts (internal) 1 if there was an internal conflict in a given year in a given area, 0 otherwise
10. Time period
	- 1500-2000.
11. Geographical coverage
	- Entire World.
12. Methodologies used for data collection and processing
- The dataset were submitted by the author Peter Brecke to the Institutions hub at the CGEH  and the information contained in it has been converted to the CLIO INFRA template by Peter Foldvari.
13. Data quality
		i. Central statistical agencies
		ii. Historical reconstructions
		iii. Estimates
		iv. Conjectures
Based on available historical information on wars and conflicts. The work is still on progress, accuracy is not 100%. Some further mistakes might have been made during the conversion.

14. Period of collection
	- October/2010 and July/2012.
15. Data collectors
	- Peter Brecke
16. Sources
Peter Brecke: Violent Conflicts 1400 A.D. to the Present  in Different Regions of the World

17. Text
The current dataset has been made using the Conflict Cataloge dataset by Peter Brecke who was so kind as to submit his data to the institutions hub of the CGEH in Utrecht.
