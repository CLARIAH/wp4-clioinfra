1. Title

	Heights by birth decade and country, both before and after 1800

2. Author(s)

	Baten, Joerg, University of Tuebingen.; Blum, Mathias, Technical
University Munich (formerly University of Tuebingen)

3. Production date

	2013-12-26.

4. Version

	1

5. Variable group(s)

	Biological Standard of Living

6. Variable(s)

	Male height equivalent in cm.

7. Unit of analysis

	Country

8. Keywords (5)

	Height, Anthropometrics, Living Standard.

9. Abstract (200 words)

	This variable contains anthropometric information which can be used as
an indicator for human health and welfare in 165 countries spanning the
period 1810-1989, and a smaller number of countries 1500-1800 

10. Time period

	1500 -1980. All data refer to the birth decadal average (1810 means
1810-19 etc), except those before 1800, which sometimes refer to half
centuries

11. Geographical coverage

	Worldwide

12. Methodologies used for data collection and processing

	Reconstruction of heights by birth decade using a variety of different
sources. See also the text below.

13. Data quality

	As good as possible, but counter-checking and improvement welcome. 

14. Date of collection

	See references

15. Data collectors

	Joerg Baten, Mathias Blum, and many colleagues from around the world.
